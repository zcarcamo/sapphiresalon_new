Kat

Kat is the owner and operator of Sapphire Salon. Kat can’t remember a time she hasn’t loved hair and makeup, with her interest originating all the way back to her childhood. She has cultivated her love of hair into over ten years of salon and barbering experience. She has an unbridled passion for her craft with a focus on continued education. Kat is a Joico educator and specializes in fashion colors, men’s services and styling.

call or text 402.318.6843 for an appointment

Brie

Brie has close to 20 years of hairstyling experience, with her interest in hair emerging from always doing her friends’ hair and makeup in high school. While originally pursuing a career in healthcare, Brie ventured back to her passion for hair shortly after her son was born. She offers a wide breadth of services including cuts, colors, waxing, and extensions. She likes to impart a lot of texture in her cuts, especially for those with short to medium-length hair.

call or text 402.432.3488 for an appointment

Kelly

Kelly has over 14 years of hairstyling experience under her belt. She focuses on color cut hand-tied and taped in extensions, Brazillian blowouts, and formal styling. Somewhat ironically, her interest in hairstyling was piqued from a horrible haircut she received as a child. She has since parlayed that interest into a dynamic career in the beauty industry. Her favorite services to provide are hair coloring, formal styling and doing extensions.

call or text 402.580.0628 for an appointment

Autumn

For Autumn, hairstyling essentially runs in her blood. With a passion inherited from her hairstylist grandma, the hair salon served as a sort of playground for Autumn growing up. She fell in love with the environment of the salon early on, and the rest is history. Beyond cuts and colors, Autumn specializes in extensions, formal styling, as well as toning and deep conditioning treatments.

call or text 402.806.3102 for an appointment

Leah

Leah is the most recent addition to Sapphire Salon. She has over 12 years of hairstyling experience, spurring from a lifetime love of the fashion industry. Even from a young age, Leah has experimented with doing everyone’s hair growing up. She loves boosting her clients’ confidence and the meaningful relationships she has cultivated with her clients. Leah offers a wide array of services, including lash lifts, color corrections, and men’s cuts.
