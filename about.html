Hair that reflects you

Sapphire Salon strives to create hair that reflects you. Our customers know precisely what they need, and come to us to achieve their #hairgoals. Regardless of whether it’s long charming waves, the latest fashion color, or a multi-textured bob, we can deliver what you crave. Our number one priority is the health of your hair and our dedicated stylists are constantly scouring the latest technologies to get you hair polished and healthy, using meticulous application techniques.

Taking the intimidation out of going to the hair salon, Sapphire’s space offers a sleek, modern take on boho chic. It has a calming, eclectically botanical vibe where we create haircuts, styles and barbering services that reflect the best version of yourself.
